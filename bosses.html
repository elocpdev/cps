Bosses

Each floor in the Catacombs is defended by a boss, including the entrance. As you progress in the game, you go to lower floors, until the seventh, where there are four wither bosses.

The Watcher

The Watcher used to be a human called Lathrop. He was orphaned in the Calamity and was adopted by scientist Dr. Emmett. Ever since he was little, he could see visions that seemed to be able to predict the future.

One day, Lathrop was sent to deliver a letter and stayed in an inn overnight. This was the night when he was brought to the Wither King and commanded to look into an orb called the seeing-stone. Through the orb, Lathrop saw Kaeman’s past, which he then wrote into the journal “Memories”.

The Wither King was very pleased with Lathrop’s initial results and commanded him to look into the orb every day, until Lathrop was completely exhausted. His limbs were removed to reach his full potential, and his eyes merged into one. Lathrop was now the Watcher.

Livid

As said in the timeline, Livid was “kidnapped” by Necron and brought to the Catacombs after a failed assassination. Livid joined the Guild of Thieves when he was fifteen, and his skills took him not only to the top ranks of the guild but also to a more exciting job.

Being an excellent assassin, Livid quickly rose to the top again and often boasted about it to other guild members. They were all fed up with it, but if they spoke against him, they would be the ones killed.

Livid quickly got bored of his kills and looked for more high-profile prey. This led him to the inn where he would find a “merchant”, the perfect target. Unfortunately, the merchant turned out to be Necron, who was intrigued by Livid’s skills and took him to train in the Catacombs.

Sadan

Sadan was one of the few mages who were sent on the expedition in 1531 AE. He was the only survivor from the whole group, as Necron saw talent in him and decided to take him in as a student. Sadan would later find out that Necron’s fellow wither-necromancer, Storm, was his father, and would dedicate his training to try get Storm’s approval.

Necron

Necron was orphaned as a result of the Wither King’s attacks. He was adopted by the Academy’s headmaster and joined the Academy, but he never succeeded in producing a simple spell, much less passing the exams. He later found that this was because he had an affinity for dark magic, which was forbidden.

One day, Necron was lashing out in his room due to threats of expulsion, when someone slid a scroll under his door. This scroll turned out to be a spell, dark magic that was supposed to be sealed up after Kaeman’s rampage. Necron tried the spell and instantly realized that he could use Fel magic.

Using the spell, Necron snuck to the Wither King’s cage as he needed to learn more. The Wither King took interest in him, for one favour: to free the Wither King. Over the days, Necron practiced his magic and eventually cast a spell to blow up the cage. The Wither King quickly grabbed Necron and jumped into the void, making everyone else assume that they died, but in reality, there was an entrance to the Catacombs at the edge of the island.
